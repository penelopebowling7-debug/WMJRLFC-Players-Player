Choose your team

Select the team you are voting for. The current round opens automatically based on the coach settings.

By continuing, you confirm parent or guardian consent to use player names inside this app for positive recognition, award tracking, and club reporting.

How voting works

  • Select 3 different players.
  • Your choices count as 3 points, 2 points, and 1 point.
  • Each browser can submit once per team per round in this demo.
  • Coaches can open or rename the round from the coach portal.

Cast your votes

Tap one score per player. You must select exactly one 3-point vote, one 2-point vote, and one 1-point vote.

Review your selections

    Please check your choices before submitting. In this demo app, a browser can only vote once per team and round.

    Thanks - your vote has been recorded.
