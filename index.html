About the Player's Player Award

This award is chosen by the players.

It is not just about scoring tries or winning games.

It is about:

  • Being a good team mate
  • Trying your best
  • Helping others
  • Showing sportsmanship
  • Being fair and kind
  • Supporting your team

Choose the player who showed the best team spirit.

Coach Team Setup

Instructions for Coaches

  • Create your team once only.
  • Enter your team name exactly as you want it shown.
  • If there is more than one team in the same age group, include the team colour in the name. Example: U12's Black.
  • Enter your own coach name.
  • Choose a coach PIN you will remember.
  • Enter one player name per line.
  • You can add, edit, remove or restore players later in Coach Access.
