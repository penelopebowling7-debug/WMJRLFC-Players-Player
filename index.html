WMJRLFC Player's Player Voting

A club-branded, mobile-friendly voting app that gives parents a simple vote flow, coaches self-service team and player setup, and club admins a clearer overview with live Firestore-backed data.

Connecting to Firebase…
Club colours and logo Firestore data sync Coach self-service Admin reporting

Vote Now

Parents and guardians can choose the team, rank 3 players, and submit a vote in a guided flow with review and confirmation.

Coach Portal

Coaches can create their own team, manage players, control the round, and view live tallies without needing admin setup.

Club Admin

Club admins can see participation, monitor all teams, create coaches if needed, and export live data for reporting.

Choose your team

Voting follows the round that the coach has opened for the team.

By proceeding, you confirm parent or guardian consent to use player names for Player's Player voting, team recognition, and club reporting.

How voting works

  • Choose 3 different players.
  • Your selections count as 3 points, 2 points, and 1 point.
  • Only open rounds can receive votes.
  • This version records a unique browser vote per team and round using Firestore.

Cast your votes

Assign one score to each of your top 3 players. You can tap again to unselect and change your choice.

Review your selections

    Please check your choices before submitting. Once sent, this browser cannot vote again for the same team and round.

    Thanks — your vote has been recorded.

    The team tally has been updated in Firestore. Coaches and admins will see the updated data when they refresh their dashboard.
