About Players' Player Voting

Each game, players select one teammate who they believe showed great sportsmanship, team-first behaviour, and their best effort. This app helps parents support their children to submit one vote each round. By using this app, you agree to the storage of player names for club reward and recognition purposes.

Player Voting

Choose your team

Select your team and round, then continue to vote.

1. Choose one player only.
2. Each player can vote once per round.
3. Players must identify themselves before voting.
4. Players cannot vote for themselves.

Who is voting?

Cast your vote
