Players' Player recognises the teammate who showed the best effort, attitude, teamwork, and sportsmanship in that round. Players complete one vote each round, with parents helping younger children submit their choice through the app when needed.

How voting works

  • Choose one player only.
  • Each player can vote once per round.
  • Only unlocked rounds can receive votes.
  • Players must identify themselves before voting.
  • Players cannot vote for themselves.
